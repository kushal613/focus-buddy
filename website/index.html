The Science Behind Focus Warmup

Our approach is grounded in cognitive science and behavioral psychology research.

üß† Cognitive Load Theory

Research by John Sweller shows that our working memory has limited capacity. Focus Warmup uses micro-learning (2-3 sentences) to avoid cognitive overload while still delivering meaningful content.

‚è±Ô∏è Spaced Repetition

Based on Hermann Ebbinghaus's forgetting curve, we strategically time learning sessions to reinforce concepts when you're most likely to forget them.
